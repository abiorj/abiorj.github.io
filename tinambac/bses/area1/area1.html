Compliance Report
Parameter A: STATEMENT OF VISION, MISSION, GOALS AND OBJECTIVES
Systems-Input and Processes
S.1. The institution has a system for determining the Vision and Mission.
S.2. The Vision clearly reflects what the institution hopes to become in the future.
S.3. The Mission clearly reflects the institution's legal and educational mandate.
S.4. The Goals are clearly stated, and are consistent with the Mission of the Institution.
S.5. The Objectives of the program clearly state the expected outcomes in terms of competencies (skills and knowledge), values and other attributes of the graduates which include the development of:

S.5.1. technical skillls in Hotel and Restaurant Management/TM/HM/TrM;

S.5.2. scientific habit of thought;

S.5.3. entry-level competencies in HRM/TMHM/TrM;

S.5.4. research and extension capabilities

S.5.5. entrepreneurial skills;

S.5.6. students' own ideas, desirable attitudes, personal discipline and moral character;

S.5.7. critical thinking skills; and

S.5.8. aesthetic and cultural values.
Implementation
I.1. The institution/ College conduct a review on the statement of the Vision and Mission as well as its goals and program objectives for the approval of authorities concerned.
I.2.The College / Academic Unit follow a system of formulating its goal and the objectives of the program.
I.3.The College/academic Unit faculty, personnel, students and other stakeholders (cooperating agencies, linkages, alumni, industry sector and other concerned groups) participate in the formulation, review and/or revision of the VMGO.
Outcome/s
O.1. The VMGO are crafted and duly approved by the BOT/BOR.
Parameter B: DISSEMINATION AND ACCEPTABILITY
Systems-Input and Processes
S.1. The VMGO are available on bulletin boards, in catalogs/manuals and in other form of communication media.
Implementation
I.1. A system of dissemination and acceptability of the VMGO is enforced.
I.2. The administrators/faculty attends in-service seminars and training on awareness and acceptability of the:

I.2.1. Vision and mission of the institution;

I.2.2. Goals of the College / Academic Unit; and

I.2.3. Objectives of the program.
I.3. The formulation/review/revision of the VMGO is participated in by the following:

I.2.1. administrators;

I.3.2. faculty;

I.3.3. staff;

I.3.4. atudents; and

I.3.5. other stakeholder;
I.4. The faculty and staff perform their jobs/functions in consonance with the VMGO.
I.5. The VMGO are widely disseminated to the different agencies, institutions, industry sector, and the community.
Outcome/s
O.1. There is full awareness and acceptance of the VMGO by the administrators, faculty, staff, students, and other stakeholders.
O.2. There is congruency between actual educational practices and activities with the following:

O.2.1. Vision and Mission of SUC;

O.2.2. Goals of the College/Department of HRM/TM/HM/TrM; and

O.2.3. Objectives of the HRM/TM/HM/TrM program.
O.3. The goals and objectives are being achieved.
